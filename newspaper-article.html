Our children saw "The Little Mermaid"
Michaelle Straßegger

"Far out at sea, the water is as blue as the most beautiful cornflowers and as clear as the purest glass…" So begins perhaps the most famous fairy tale by Hans Christian Andersen, which has become a true classic.

During a storm, Marie saved a young human prince from drowning and fell in love with him. Her longing to see him again and her curiosity about his world are so great that she engages in a dangerous bargain. She gives the old sea witch her enchanting voice in exchange for a chance at life on land.
