Grießkoch from Austria - vegan Version

Every one in Austria loves this special pulp or pudding.

Ingredients

  • Weizengrieß - Semolina
  • plant based milk
  • Salt
  • Cinnamon and sugar

Instructions

  1. Put your milk in a big pot and let it boil.
  2. Put salt in it and your Semolina and cook until it is viscous.
  3. Mix Cinnamon and sugar 1:1 together.
  4. Put your Grießkoch on plates and put the mixture over the pulp.
